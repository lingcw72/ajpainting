Before you book

Frequently asked questions

Do you provide a free quotation?

Yes. A&J provides free on-site quotations for all property types. Viewing appointments are available Monday to Friday, 11am–4pm.

Can you paint an occupied home?

Yes. We regularly work in lived-in homes. The team plans furniture movement and protection carefully and maintains usable access wherever practical.

What preparation is included?

The exact scope is confirmed during quotation. Typical work includes wall inspection, crack and screw-hole repair, masking, furniture moving and covering, floor protection and colour consultation.

Do you use subcontractors?

A&J does not subcontract residential painting. Our regular painting team handles preparation through completion. Electrical, plumbing, air-conditioning, carpentry, tiling and renovation work may be arranged through external partners.

Which paint brands and coats do you use?

We use premium Nippon Paint and Dulux products. Light colours normally receive at least two coats; dark colours may require three to five. Oil- or water-based primers are applied where the surface requires them.

Is there a workmanship warranty?

Yes. Warranty coverage and rectification terms depend on the scope and are explained during quotation and stated on the invoice.

Which parts of Singapore do you serve?

We serve homeowners across Singapore. Contact us with your postal code and project details to confirm availability.
