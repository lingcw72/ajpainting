Before you book

Frequently asked questions

Do you provide a free quotation?

Yes. A&J provides free on-site quotations for HDB and condo units by appointment. Send your property type, location and preferred timing on WhatsApp to get started.

Can you paint an occupied home?

Yes. We regularly work in lived-in homes. The team plans furniture movement and protection carefully and maintains usable access wherever practical.

What preparation is included?

The exact scope is confirmed during quotation. Typical preparation may include masking, covering, patching screw holes or crack lines, light surface correction and cleaning before painting.

Do you use subcontractors?

A&J is a direct contractor. The painters are trained and managed directly, so responsibility and communication stay with the team you engaged.

Which parts of Singapore do you serve?

We serve homeowners across Singapore. Contact us with your postal code and project details to confirm availability.
