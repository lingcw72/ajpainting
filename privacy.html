Privacy & data protection

Personal Data Protection Policy

Effective: 23 July 2026

A&J Painting And Handyman Services (“A&J”, “we”, “us” or “our”), UEN / Registration No. 53102106L, respects your personal data and handles it in accordance with Singapore’s Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (“PDPA”).

1. Personal data we may receive

When you voluntarily contact us by telephone, email or WhatsApp, we may receive information such as your name, telephone number, email address, property location, property type, project requirements, preferred dates, photographs and other information you choose to provide.

The quotation form on this website does not submit information to our web server. It prepares a message on your device and opens WhatsApp. You decide whether to send that message.

2. Why we use personal data

We may collect, use or disclose personal data where reasonably necessary to:

  • respond to enquiries and prepare quotations;
  • arrange viewings, painting work and related services;
  • communicate about appointments, payments, work progress and after-service matters;
  • maintain business and transaction records;
  • handle requests, feedback, disputes or safety matters; and
  • comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations.

If we wish to use your personal data for a materially different purpose, we will notify you and obtain consent where required.

3. Disclosure and external services

We do not sell personal data. We may disclose relevant information to workers, service providers or professional advisers only where reasonably necessary for the purposes described above, or where required or authorised by law. Anyone handling personal data on our behalf should receive only what is necessary and protect it appropriately.

WhatsApp, Facebook and Google are external services governed by their own privacy terms. When you follow an external link or send a WhatsApp message, that provider may process information, including outside Singapore, according to its policies.

4. Website storage, cookies and technical data

This website does not currently set cookies and does not use advertising pixels or web analytics. It stores only your selected language preference in your browser’s local storage. You may remove this preference through your browser settings. The site loads fonts from Google; as with most web requests, technical information such as your IP address may be received by the provider when the font files are requested.

If analytics, advertising or other tracking technology is introduced later, this policy and any necessary consent controls will be updated before that technology is activated.

5. Retention and protection

We retain personal data only for as long as it is reasonably needed for the purpose for which it was collected, for legitimate business or legal purposes, or as required by law. When it is no longer required, we will take reasonable steps to delete it, anonymise it or otherwise cease retaining it.

We take reasonable administrative, physical and technical measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, collection, use, disclosure, copying, modification, loss or disposal. No internet or messaging service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

6. Access, correction and withdrawal of consent

You may contact us to ask about personal data under our control, request correction of inaccurate data, or withdraw consent for future use where applicable. We may need to verify your identity and will handle the request in accordance with the PDPA. Withdrawal does not affect uses already permitted by law and may limit our ability to provide requested services.

7. Privacy enquiries

For privacy questions, requests or complaints, contact:

8. Policy changes

We may update this policy when our practices, services or legal obligations change. The effective date above will be revised when a material update is published.
